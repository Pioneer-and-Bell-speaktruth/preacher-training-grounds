Expository Sermons

What It Is

An expository sermon explains and applies a passage of Scripture in its context. The main point of the text becomes the main point of the sermon.

How to Build One

1. Choose a passage

2. Study the context

3. Identify the big idea

4. Break the text into movements

5. Explain → Illustrate → Apply

Example Outline

Title: Standing Firm in the Faith

Text: 1 Peter 5:6–11

Point 1: Humble Yourself (v. 6)

Point 2: Cast Your Cares (v. 7)

Point 3: Resist the Devil (vv. 8–9)
