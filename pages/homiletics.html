Homiletics Basics

What Is Homiletics?

Homiletics is the craft of preaching—shaping a sermon so that truth is clear, compelling, and faithful to Scripture.

Core Elements

• Structure: clear beginning, middle, and end.

• Clarity: one big idea, clear points.

• Transitions: smooth movement between points.

• Application: specific, honest, Christ-centered.

• Delivery: voice, pace, posture, eye contact.

Checklist

□ Can I state the big idea in one sentence?

□ Do my points grow out of the text?

□ Are my applications concrete, not vague?

□ Am I preaching scripture, not just advice?
