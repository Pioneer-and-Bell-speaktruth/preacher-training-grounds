Topical Sermons

What It Is

A topical sermon begins with a subject (e.g., faith, hope, love) and gathers Scriptures that teach on that topic.

How to Build One

1. Choose a biblical topic

2. Gather Scriptures responsibly

3. Organize the teaching logically

4. Keep Christ at the center

Example Outline

Topic: Encouragement

Point 1: God Encourages Us (2 Cor 1:3–4)

Point 2: We Encourage One Another (Heb 10:24–25)

Point 3: The Scriptures Encourage Us (Rom 15:4)
