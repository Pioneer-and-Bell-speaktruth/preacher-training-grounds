Textual Sermons

What It Is

A textual sermon takes one verse or a short passage and builds the sermon from key phrases or ideas within that verse.

How to Build One

1. Choose a strong, clear verse

2. Identify the natural divisions

3. Build points from the text itself

4. Keep the sermon anchored to the verse

Example Outline

Text: Micah 6:8

Point 1: Do Justly

Point 2: Love Mercy

Point 3: Walk Humbly
