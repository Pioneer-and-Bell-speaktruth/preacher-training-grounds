Preacher's Heart

Why the Heart Matters

God cares not only what we say, but who we are. The preacher's heart must be humble, prayerful, and honest before God.

Key Traits

• Humility: God-centered, not self-centered.

• Prayer: dependence before preparation and delivery.

• Integrity: the same person in private and public.

• Compassion: loving people, not just preaching at them.

• Courage: saying what God says, even when hard.

Heart Check

□ Am I praying and not performing?

□ Am I confessing sin and walking in the light?

□ Do I love the people I preach to?

□ Am I willing to obey the text myself?
