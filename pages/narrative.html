Narrative Sermons

What It Is

A narrative sermon follows the flow of a biblical story, highlighting the tension, turning point, and resolution.

How to Build One

1. Identify the plot structure

2. Highlight the conflict

3. Show God's action

4. Apply the story to today

Example Outline

Story: David & Goliath (1 Samuel 17)

Movement 1: The Challenge

Movement 2: The Faith

Movement 3: The Victory
